The Day

Rundown

From the first seat to the final toast, the day in order.

  1. 09:00

    Doors open — guests seated

    Light refreshments served in the foyer. Ushers will assist with seating.

  2. 09:30

    Processional

    Faculty and the Class of 2026 enter the hall in formal procession.

  3. 10:00

    Welcome & Keynote

    Address by Dean Holloway, followed by the keynote — "On Beginnings" — by Dr. Naomi Reyes.

  4. 10:45

    Recognition of Graduates

    Names read aloud by department. Graduates rise to be acknowledged by their families.

  5. 11:15

    Conferral of Degrees

    The Dean confers degrees upon the Class of 2026. Caps are moved.

  6. 11:45

    Closing Remarks

    Words from the Class President, then the Benediction by the University Chaplain.

  7. 12:00

    Recessional

    Faculty retire. Graduates exit to applause and the toss on the South Lawn.

  8. 17:00

    Evening Reception — the Atrium

    Music, light fare, and the company of everyone who made the years mean something.
